"The Panchayat Sandesh"

Panchayat Sandesh is the official magazine of the All India Panchayat Parishad. It is a non-political and non-governmental organization established in 1958 in Jasidih, Bihar. The organization primarily focuses on the upliftment of the three-tier Panchayati Raj institutions (Village Panchayat, Block Panchayat, and District Panchayat) in India. It was founded to bring Mahatma Gandhi's concept of Gram Swaraj (village self-rule) to reality.
