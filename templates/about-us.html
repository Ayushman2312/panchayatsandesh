About Us

Welcome to The Panchayat Sandesh, a leading monthly magazine upholding the "Gram Swaraj" vision of Mahatma Gandhi. We focus on the challenges and achievements of Panchayats and Rural India, serving as a dedicated platform for rural development, governance, and community empowerment.

Established with the vision to spread nationwide awareness about Panchayats, we are the cornerstone of our vibrant Democracy, amplifying voices from the grassroots. Our magazine serves as a platform for Panchayats and rural communities to share their stories, challenges, and successes.

Our Mission

At Panchayat Sandesh, our mission is to promote inclusive growth by highlighting the initiatives, policies, and innovations that are transforming rural India. We aim to empower local governance bodies and citizens by fostering awareness, transparency, and participation in decision-making processes.

  • • Rural Development: Success stories of rural initiatives and government schemes
  • • Agriculture and Livelihoods: Insights into modern agricultural practices
  • • Education and Health: Features on improving access to quality services
  • • Environment and Sustainability: Articles on eco-friendly practices
  • • Women and Youth Empowerment: Showcasing inspiring success stories

Our Audience

We cater to a diverse audience that includes Panchayat members, rural entrepreneurs, social workers, academicians, policy analysts, government officials, and anyone interested in the development of India's rural landscape. Through thought-provoking articles, interviews, and case studies, Panchayat Sandesh strives to be a resource for those working at the grassroots level to create lasting change.

Join Us

At The Panchayat Sandesh, we believe in the power of collaboration. We invite contributors, experts, and rural changemakers to share their insights and experiences with us. Together, we can drive meaningful progress for India's rural communities.

Stay connected with us as we continue our journey of bringing the voices of rural India to the forefront!

Panchayat Sandesh is the official magazine of the All India Panchayat Parishad, dedicated to empowering local governance and rural development.
